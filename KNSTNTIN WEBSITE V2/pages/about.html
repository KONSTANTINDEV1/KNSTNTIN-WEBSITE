Cases

ARTIST | PRODUCER | INSTRUMENTALIST

My journey has taken many roads during my career as a musician, working within orchestras as a trombonist, Steel pan and Samba groups as a percussionist as well as a vocalist in several choral choirs. My knowledge of these aspects has now led me down in to the realms of fusion and experimentation as an Artist-Producer to date. Today I regularly explore the use of cross-pollination and genre hybridity as a means to pull from my Classical, Jazz and Hip-hop rootes to form something new every time, always thinking of ways to create immersive and organic soundscapes.

Currently performing on keys with up and coming artist Joe Garratt in Leeds, I have also had the pleasure of working with several amazing artists and musicians to both record and produce their demos and singles. These include; The Wardrobe Quintet , Eva Eik, DOMI, Black Sapphire and ITV (What am I made of) Inaya Folarin. My aim is to continue to explore the amazing talent that is out there, collaborating and recording for others as well as myself, constantly seeking innovative material. If you would like me to produce your next track please contact me via the contact page.
